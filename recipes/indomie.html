Indomie Noodles(The Nigerian Style)

A properly prepared Nigerian Indomie Noodles

Ingredients

  • Indomie
  • Vegetable oil
  • Carrot
  • Onions
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Green beans
  • Fresh pepper

Steps

  1. Put a moderate quatity of water in the pot(depending on the number of packs) and boil for about 10 minutes
  2. Open the indomie from the pack and put it inside the pot then allow to boil for another 10 minutes
  3. After 10 minutes, dry out the remaining water
  4. Slice your onions, green beans, carrots, fresh tomatoes and pepper to your taste
  5. Put a little amount of vegetable amount of oil in the pan, then add your sliced onions, carrots, green beans, fresh tomatoes and pepper
  6. Fry for 5 minutes then add your seasoning to it also add a little quantity of water and allow to boil for 5 minutes
  7. Add your boiled indomie into the pan and stir properly for proper mixing
  8. Viola! Your indomie is ready
