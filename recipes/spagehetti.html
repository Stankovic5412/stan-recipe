Spaghetti(The Nigerian Style)

Nigerian prepared Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Vegetable oil
  • Carrot
  • Onions
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Green beans
  • Fresh pepper
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Crayfish

Steps

  1. Put a moderate quatity of water in the pot(depending on the number of packs) and boil for about 10 minutes
  2. Open the Spaghetti from the pack and put it inside the pot then allow to boil for another 10 minutes
  3. After 10 minutes, dry out the remaining water
  4. Slice your onions, carrots, green beans, fresh tomatoes and pepper to your taste
  5. Put a moderate amount of vegetable amount of oil in the pan, then add your sliced onions, green beans, carrots, fresh tomatoes and pepper
  6. Fry for 10 minutes then add a half teaspoom of curry and thyme to it
  7. Then add your crayfish and allow to boil for 5 minutes
  8. Add your boiled Spaghetti into the pan and stir properly for proper mixing then allow to boil for 5 minutes
  9. Viola! Your Spaghetti is ready
